Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-01

Musiq Studio (“we”, “us”) integrates with Pi Network. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Pi account info (e.g., username) after you grant permission in Pi Browser.
  • Usage data (basic logs) stored locally during testing.
  • Audio you load or record, which stays local unless you later upload/mint.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate via Pi SDK (sandbox for testing).
  • Enable features (load, record, sandbox payments).
  • Show “minted items” locally for demo purposes.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. During sandbox/testing, data stays on-device unless you explicitly submit it to a backend (not enabled yet).
